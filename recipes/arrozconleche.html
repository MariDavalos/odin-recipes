Arroz con leche

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups of water
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • 2 Cloves
  • 1 Cup of Long Rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Vanilla Essence
  • 1 Can of IDEAL® CREMOSITA
  • 1 can of NESTLÉ® CONDENSED MILK
  • 1/3 Cup Raisins
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Place the water in a pot, then add the cinnamon and cloves. Once the water starts to boil, remove the cinnamon and cloves and add the previously washed rice. Let it cook over medium heat so that it cooks.
  2. Stir with the help of a fork to avoid breaking the rice grains. When it is cooked and the water has been consumed, remove the cinnamon and the cloves.
  3. Add the vanilla essence, the IDEAL® CREMOSITA and the NESTLÉ® CONDENSED MILK.
  4. Cook over medium heat for 5 to 8 minutes. Cook over medium heat for 5 to 8 minutes, until you see that the consistency is not so light.
  5. Add raisins, decorate with cinnamon to taste and serve.
